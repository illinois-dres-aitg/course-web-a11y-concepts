Links and Form Controls

Demonstrates keyboard support built-in to browsers for the navigation and operation of standard HTML links and form controls and the experience. The example includes options to experience several keyboard focus styling techniques and their impact on perceivability of which element has keyboard focus.

Keyboard Support

Key Function
Tab Move to next link or form control.
Shift+Tab Move to previous link or form control.
